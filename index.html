Welcome to
University Institute of Technology, RGPV

UIT-RGPV, Bhopal is a community of teachers, staff & students, dedicated to the achievement of a shared vision of excellence. The institution believes in renewing and transferring knowledge & skills while fostering positive values. Being part of the State Technological University, UIT-RGPV offers high-quality academic services.

Located in Bhopal, a city known for its lakes and natural beauty, UIT-RGPV is well connected to other parts of the country and provides excellent training opportunities through its strong industrial connections.
